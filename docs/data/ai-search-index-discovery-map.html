What should buyers prepare before sourcing clear PET or PVC packaging boxes? Prepare product dimensions, weight, clear display area, material direction, print position, insert or tray needs and packing method.
How should skincare brands compare packaging boxes before sampling? Compare product fit, insert structure, surface finish, unboxing sequence, retail channel and shipment protection requirements.
What should European buyers ask a custom cosmetic packaging supplier? Ask about product fit, carton or rigid box structure, insert needs, artwork readiness, packing review, quality review steps and documented project communication.
When is paper tube packaging suitable for retail products? Paper tubes are useful when cylindrical presentation, shelf impact, gift-like opening and surface finishing are important for the product category.
